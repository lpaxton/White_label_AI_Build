Welcome to aRCHi [Intelligence]

aRCHi [Intelligence] is a suite of tools designed to help you extract, neutralize, compare, and edit article content with ease. Whether you're looking to clean up an article for review or transform it into a brand-neutral version, aRCHi has you covered.

01

Article Extractor

Extract and clean article content from any URL. Perfect for reviewing and processing article text.

  • Extract <article> content from URLs
  • Remove unwanted elements (links, call-outs)
  • Clean, readable text output
  • Real-time content preview
Open
02

Brand Neutralizer

Transform brand-specific content into generic, white-label alternatives using AI for intelligent rewriting.

  • AI-powered brand neutralization
  • Automatic trademark removal
  • Smart product name replacement
  • Validation and warning detection
  • HTML structure preservation
Open
03

Content Diff Tool

Compare original article content against brand-neutralized output side by side to verify every change made by the AI.

  • Side-by-side rendered view
  • Word-level diff highlighting
  • Original vs. rewritten markers
  • Synchronized scrolling
  • Change count statistics
Open
04

Word Editor

Review and refine the brand-neutralized article in a word-processor-style editor before final export.

  • Rich text editing (bold, italic, lists…)
  • Heading & paragraph formatting
  • Remove AI highlight markers
  • Live word & character count
  • Download as HTML or plain text
Open
KB

Knowledge Base Chat

Ask questions and get answers drawn exclusively from the articles stored in the FCAT database. No outside knowledge — built for regulatory compliance.

  • Answers grounded solely in the DB
  • Semantic article retrieval per query
  • Source article citations per response
  • Conversation history support
  • Zero outside-knowledge contamination
Open
Enhanced

Enhanced Persona Finder

Advanced persona finder with both AI and smart keyword-based matching options.

  • Smart Search (no AI required)
  • AI Analysis (with Ollama)
  • CORS issue workarounds
  • Dual-mode functionality
Open
New

Article Generator

Generate new articles in your writing style using your existing content as examples. Combines Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) with Ollama.

  • Uses your own articles as examples
  • RAG-powered: finds similar content for context
  • Ollama LLM for generation
  • Interactive and script modes
  • Style analysis and creativity controls
Open
