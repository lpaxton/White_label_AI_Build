Welcome to Article Extractor!

This tool helps you extract clean, readable content from web articles.

How it works:

  1. Enter the URL of an article you want to extract
  2. Choose your cleaning options (remove links, call-outs, etc.)
  3. Click "Extract Article Content"
  4. Review the clean, formatted content
  5. Click the "Send to Brand Neutralizer" button or save as a text or HTML file if needed.

Perfect for: Content analysis, research, cleaning up articles for better readability
